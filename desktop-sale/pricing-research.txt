WHAT CHANGED SINCE MARCH 2
==========================
- DDR5 crisis escalated, NOT eased. 64GB kits roughly doubled again at
  retail. Samsung doubled contract DRAM in Dec 2025; forecasts point
  to another +30–50% through Q2 2026.
- RTX 4090 FE softened ~5–10% from Feb/Mar peak. Panic buying cooled
  but 5090 supply still structurally tight (memory/AI demand), so the
  floor remains well above MSRP. Hardwareswap asks still clustering
  $2,030–$2,375 — not a collapse.
- NAND caught up with DRAM — Gen5 SSDs now carry a small used premium.
- CPU, mobo, PSU, case, cooler: essentially flat.

Net: DDR5 gain (+$300) partially offsets 4090 softening (-$400). Total
recovery is ~$78 lower than March; composition shifted more toward RAM.
